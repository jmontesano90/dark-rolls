Thank you for your interest in Dark Rolls!

This site is designed to assist any players trying to make more informed decisions while playing The Dark Souls Board game.
More often than not it can be hard to determine what a better piece of equipment is dude to varying factors.
Are 2 blues with a -1 better than 3 black dice? This site hopes to answer just that.
Simply put in what dice your equipment has and any additional modifiers and click submit.
5000 rolls will be simulated with your dice of choice and the accompaying statistics will be calculated and shown to you.
Site works well on mobile and desktop.
